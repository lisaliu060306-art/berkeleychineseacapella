✨ 关于伯村清唱团

“让中文旋律，在纯然人声中绽放”

我们是伯村清唱团 (BCA)，加州大学伯克利分校首支混声中文阿卡贝拉团体。因对中文音乐的热爱而相聚，我们用纯粹的人声编织经典与现代的旋律，在无伴奏的和声中诉说文化的温度。每一首歌，都是我们对中华文化的致敬与分享。

15+位常驻歌手 · 5个声部 (SATB+VP)

✨ About Berkeley Chinese Acapella

“Where Mandarin melodies meet golden harmonies”

We are Berkeley Chinese A Cappella (BCA), UC Berkeley's premier co-ed Chinese a cappella group. Born from a shared passion for Chinese music, we weave together timeless Mandarin melodies with the pure, intimate power of the human voice. Through our music, we celebrate and share the richness of Chinese culture—one harmony at a time.
